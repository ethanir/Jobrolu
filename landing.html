What is Jobrolu

What it is

Not another job board.

Job boards make you scroll thousands of listings and judge each one from a title. Jobrolu does the reading for you. It pulls every open engineering role, has an AI read each full posting against your background, and surfaces only the ones that genuinely fit, with the reasons spelled out.

It reads the whole posting

Each full job description goes to an AI that weighs your stack, seniority, and experience the way a careful recruiter would, instead of matching keywords in the title.

500+ companies, always fresh

Roles come straight from company career systems like Greenhouse, Lever, Ashby, and Workday, and refresh on their own, so the feed stays current without you lifting a finger.

Honest scores and real gaps

Every match shows why you fit, the skills you are missing, and a score you can trust, instead of a flat 99 on everything. A correct skip beats an inflated match.

You stay in control

Jobrolu finds and ranks; you apply. Every match links to the real posting and the recruiter to contact, so nothing is ever sent on your behalf.

How it works

Two passes, so nothing slips through.

A free pass ranks every role instantly. Then AI reads your best matches in full. You get speed and depth, without paying to read jobs that were never going to fit.

1

Build your profile

Upload your resume, fill in a quick form, or paste a prompt into your own AI to fill it for you. It takes about a minute, and you can edit everything before saving.

2

Every open role is scored for you, instantly

One click sends your best matches to an AI that reads each complete posting and confirms the real fits, with reasons and gaps. Verified matches are marked clearly, and you keep the recruiter link to reach out.
